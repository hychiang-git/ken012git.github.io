Hung-Yueh Chiang is a Ph.D. student in [Energy-Aware Computing Group (EnyAC)](https://enyac.org/) at The University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin) under [Prof. Diana Marculescu](https://users.ece.utexas.edu/~dianam/)'s supervision. Before joining EnyAC, Hung-Yueh was a deep learning engineer at [XYZ Robotics](https://www.xyzrobotics.ai/), a start-up company in Shanghai. From 2016 to 2018, Hung-Yueh worked with [Prof. Winston Hsu](https://winstonhsu.info/) on 3D point cloud processing and received his M.S. degree from National Taiwan University (NTU), Taipei, Taiwan in 2018. Hung-Yueh Chiang earned his B.S. degree from National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU, formerly National Chiao Tung University), Hsinchu, Taiwan in 2015.
